OUR STORY

Who Is iThemba Communications?

iThemba Communications is a telecommunications firm geared towards changing the technology industry through the provision of professional technical solutions, supply, installation and maintenance.

Technology plays a pivotal role globally in driving organisations' strategic imperatives. Global technology experts are key role players in strategic interventions, knowledgeable guidance and direction for future technological objectives.

It is at this backdrop that iThemba Communications was established — to bridge the gap between best practice and actionable results with returns on investment.
